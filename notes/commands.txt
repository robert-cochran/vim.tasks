COMMANDS

    .           in normal mode, this will repeat last command
    >           indents a selection by tab
    <           deindents
    zo          open fold
    zc          close fold
    zC          close all folds
    zO          open all folds
    ctrl+w T    opens buffer into new tab (helpful when opening help documents that splits the term)
    ctrl+w o    closes window
    ctrl+w c    closes window (yea i dunno either, one of them is probably wrong)
